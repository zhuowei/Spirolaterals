Spirolaterals, Version 0.26

Help

Make your pattern on the right match the one drawn by the computer on the left. Change your pattern by modifying the numbers with the arrow keys.
Click "Step" to draw one part, or "Start" to repeatly draw the pattern, one step at a time. click Bam to draw all of the pattern at the same time. If you need a hint, you can use a lifeline by clicking on the Hint button. You get an extra lifeline every three levels. I think you can figure out the rest.
